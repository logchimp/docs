title: LogChimp Privacy Policy
---

**Effective Date:** January 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how LogChimp collects, uses, and protects your information.

## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

**Account Information**
- Name
- Email address
- Company name (optional)

**Billing Information**
Billing details necessary to process payments
*(Note: Payment details are handled by third-party payment processors; LogChimp does not store full payment card data.)*

**Technical Information**
- IP address
- Browser and device information
- Usage logs related to the dashboard

## 2. How We Use Information

We use your information to:

- Create and manage your account
- Process license key purchases
- Provide customer support
- Improve the Service
- Communicate important updates

## 3. Self-Hosted Data

LogChimp does not collect or access content, user data, or feedback data stored in your self-hosted LogChimp instance. All such data remains under your control.

If you explicitly enable telemetry, LogChimp may collect limited, non-content technical data (such as version, feature usage, or system metadata) solely for product improvement.

Additionally, error logs or diagnostic information may be collected **only** if you choose to share them with LogChimp for troubleshooting or support purposes.

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share information with:

- Payment processors (for billing)
- Service providers assisting with infrastructure or support
- Authorities if required by law

## 5. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to:

- Maintain your account
- Comply with legal obligations
- Resolve disputes

You may request deletion of your account data, subject to legal requirements.

## 6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no system is completely secure. You use the Service at your own risk.

## 7. Your Rights

- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data

Requests can be made by contacting us.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.

## 9. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: **Email:** [opensource@codecarrot.net](mailto:opensource@codecarrot.net)
