## Posts

The most basic concept in LogChimp is the **posts** (feedback and ideas).

Posts are always tied to a specific [board](/guide/boards) and required to have a title; a description is optional but recommended.

## Votes

A user can cast or withdraw their vote on a post they have created or on someone else's feedback.

<Alert type="warning">
	**NOTE:** v0.9.0 release onward feature to vote on behalf of user is [available in Labs](/guide/dashboard/labs).
</Alert>
