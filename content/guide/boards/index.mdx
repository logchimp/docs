## Overview

Boards allow you to organize users posts. Boards can be **public** or **private**.
Public boards are visible on your LogChimp site and can be seen by anyone on the internet.

Unlike [roadmaps](/guide/roadmaps) you cannot re-arrange the order of boards.

## Dashboard

Create and/or manage boards in the **Dashboard > Boards page**.

### Create boards

<Alert type="tip">
	You need **`board:create`** permission to perform this action.
</Alert>

Creating a new board will set to private by-default. You can toggle the **Display** settings to change visibility it public.

### Update boards

<Alert type="tip">
	You need **`board:update`** permission to perform this action.
</Alert>

### Delete boards

<Alert type="tip">
	You need **`board:destroy`** permission to perform this action.
</Alert>
