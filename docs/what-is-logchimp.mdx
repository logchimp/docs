LogChimp is an open-source software built from Startups to Enterprises who need to capture customers' feedback and drive your product roadmap.

LogChimp is designed to fit and scale with your existing system by providing a unified API, feedback widget _(coming soon)_ component that can be integrated with few lines of code.

## Quick Start

- Learn more about LogChimp's architecture and key features.
- [Set up LogChimp](/docs/install) on your own servers.
- We would love to see your contributions to LogChimp. Get more information on [how to contribute](/docs/contributing).
